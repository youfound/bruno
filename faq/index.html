General
1. Who can open an account with BrunoEx?
Any individual or legitimate entity can avail of our services. However, you need to be of legal age in your jurisdiction in order to create an account with us or be a registered company in your jurisdiction.
2. How do I create my account with BrunoEx?
The registration process is very easy and will take only less than a minute. Simply click Register on the top of our website and fill in all the required fields.
3. Can I have more than one account with you?
No, you cant have more than one account per user unless you were given a special admin permission to have more. If you are flagged by the system for having more than one account, you are risking all your accounts and funds being suspended without notice.
4. Who does your team consist of?

BrunoEx Team consists of cryptocurrency traders, commodity traders, property developers, experts in the field of investments and promotions, as well as a Team of founders and leaders with significant financial and technical experience in software development and blockchain technologies.

5. What is your target audience?
Our Target Audience is an international crypto community that connects millions of people around the world into one big ecosystem. The more investment BrunoEx platform manages, the easier it is to take advantage of the market conditions, so that the returns for each member can be higher with time.
6. Where are you registered?

We are a registered entity in Azerbaijan.

7. Do you have any social media or an online group forum?

We are active on Telegram, Instagram, Threads, Twitter and Youtube.

Additionally, you can reach us through our contact us page or by emailing us at [email protected]

Investments
1. How many times can a user make deposits into their account?

There is no limitation on the number of deposits that a user can make into their account.

2. What payment methods can I use to fund my BrunoEx account?

At the moment, we are accepting USDT (BEP-20) / BTC / ETH / LTC / USDC (BEP20) / XRP / MATIC

All deposits need to be completed by pasting your transaction hash into the required field. In other case, it may take longer than usual for the deposit to reflect in your main account balance.

Note: USDT is a stablecoin which is pegged with the USD at a 1:1 ratio.

3. I am new to cryptocurrency. How can I invest?
You may buy the required cryptocurrency from our Buy/Sell section in the Home page using bank transfer, or credit/debit cards. You may copy BrunoEx crypto wallet address from your personal dashboard and paste it into the widgets receiver address section. Upon the receipt of funds in our company wallet address, your personal account balance will be updated and you may use it to make investments under available plans.
4. What is the minimum deposit and withdrawal amount?
BrunoEx has set the following minimum values for investment and withdrawal. The minimum deposit amount: USD 10 The minimum withdrawal amount: USD 10
5. How long does the deposit take to reflect in my account?

The deposit will be reflected after receiving the necessary network confirmations from the respective blockchain in most cases. However, it can take upto 24 hours for the funds to be credited into your account.

6. When will I receive my first profit earnings?

You will earn profits in line with your chosen investment plan, daily or monthly.

7. Can I withdraw/reinvest my principal investment?

You can withdraw/reinvest the principal investment at the end of term of the chosen investment plan.

8. What are the available investment plans?

STANDARD PLAN offers APY 15% with 3 months term

PREMIUM PLAN offers APY 30% with 6 months term

ULTIMATE PLAN offers APY 60% with a 1 year term

WEALTH BOOSTER PLANS:

DAILY PLAN 1.25% offers APY 50% with a 90 days term.

DAILY PLAN 0.75% offers APY 70% with a 180 days term.

DAILY PLAN 0.55% offers APY 102% with a 365 days term.

PRESTIGE PLAN (Exclusive) offers APY 151.2% with a 6 months term.

Note: APY stands for Annual Percentage Yield, and indicates the profits earned in a year. Principal is included in Daily Plans profit accruals.

9. Are there any fees?

There are no fees for deposits but withdrawals are subject to a minimum $5 or 5% management fee (whichever is greater).

All withdrawal requests received before Thursday 23:59 UTC will be processed on Friday in USDT-BEP20 only.

10. How long does it usually take for a withdrawal request to be processed?
Effective July 24, 2023 (00:00 GMT), all withdrawal requests received until Thursday (23:59 GMT) will be processed on Friday to streamline operations. In short, all withdrawals will be processed every Friday.
11. Can I withdraw my principal investment before the end of plan term?
No, you will need to wait until the end of investment term as the invested funds become part of our accumulated resource pool, used by our platform to generate profits.
12. Do you process deposit/withdrawal requests on weekends/public holidays?
You can place deposit/withdrawal requests on weekends (Saturday & Sunday/Public Holidays) but deposits will only be processed the next working day and withdrawals every Friday.
13. Can I transfer funds to my friends and family within the platform?
Yes, we have a free internal P2P fund transfer option that can be accessed from your personal dashboard.
14. When will the withdrawals be active?

The withdrawal option will only be active after the first investment is made equivalent to the deposit done inorder to avoid abuse of the bonus system.

In case the withdrawal request is made before the end of the active investment plan, the requested amount should be less than the invested amount.

Referral & Bonus
1. Do you have a referral program?

Yes, we do! You can read more about it on the Referral page. Your referral link will be displayed on your dashboard which can be shared with your family & friends.

2. What is the difference between a Deposit and an Investment?
Deposit refers to available funds in your main account balance. Investment refers to funds invested in one of our investment plans.
3. Am I eligible to receive a referral bonus without making a deposit?
Yes, you are eligible for a 5% bonus when your referral makes his/her investments without any cap on the amount or the number of investments.
4. Do I receive a bonus on my first deposit?

You will be receiving a 10% bonus on your first deposit when you register.

Note: The 10% bonus applies to your first deposit regardless of registration via a referral link.

5. Do I receive a bonus on my referrals investments?
Our platform provides you a 5% bonus on all future investments of your referrals. You may find the referral list from your personal dashboard.
6. Why should I join using a referral link?

If you register using a referral link, you get a 2.5% additional bonus on your first investment.
